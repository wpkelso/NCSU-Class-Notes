# Semantic Entailment

Semantic entailment is the second relation that is used in the [[Soundness and Completeness Theorem]].

$\Gamma \models A$ iff for every structure $\mathfrak{M}$, if $\mathfrak{M} \models \Gamma$, then $\mathfrak{M} \models A$
