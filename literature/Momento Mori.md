# Momento Mori

> [!info] Defintion
> “An artistic or symbolic trope acting as a reminder of the inevitability of death,” ([Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Memento_mori)).
> The phrase is Latin for “Remember to die.”
