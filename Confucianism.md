#culture #philosophy #china 
One of the three central philosophies of [[China]], along with [[Daoism]] and [[Legalism]].

## The Five Relationships
1. King and Minister
2. Husband and Wife
3. Father and Son
4. Elder to Younger Sibling
5. Friends

## Ren and Li
**Ren:** Human heartedness
**Li:** Ritual, rules of conduct
