# Uneven U

## Principles

1. Paragraphs are units of ideas
2. Write with different degrees of generalities: General → Specific →  More general
3. Natural voice emerges over time, through the structure of the schema

### Levels of Generality

1. “Very general”
2. “General”
3. “Intentional”
4. “Meh”
5. “Pedantry”
